Water Breathing


Water Breathing is one of the most beautiful and tranquil fighting techniques
a Breathing Style that mimics water, specifically the flow, flexibility and adaptability
of the liquid and replicates it with the user's movements, techniques and abilities.




The style consists of 10 forms in total
with the exception of one indivusal.

  7. Seventh Form: Drop Ripple Thrust

  8. Eighth Form: Waterfall Basin
